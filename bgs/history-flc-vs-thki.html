The Belgitan Conflict: FLC vs THKI

A history of Federal–Independent tensions from 3307 to the Thargoid War era.

Back in 2021 (or 3307 in ED game timeline), I was DMed by a bloke called Antraeon the self-appointed Arch-Marshal of THKI (the big man with a fancy title to match), an "independent" squadron/player minor faction (PMF). He came accusing us Feds of attacking the BGS of his PMF. I tried to politely explain to him that he was far from any of our PP1.0 bubbles and that us powerplayers had more important BGS wars to fight.

We took control of Belgitan (home of the famous Belgitan waffles 🧇 😋) and fought many PP wars over it over the years. THKI was on the doorstep of Belgitan and Dciskey negotiated a truce with Ant that we would not do any BGS on his systems as long as he did not expand further into the Belgitan bubble or try to flip systems away from Corporate control (important for our PP1.0 forting triggers).

During COVID and Thargoid War, a PP1.0 truce was declared between the Imps and us to "focus resources on the Thargoid threat". In reality this turned into an undeclared shadow PP BGS war where the Imps were purposefully encroaching Imp PMFs into our bubbles in an attempt to flip the forting triggers. During this time, Ant started to interfere in these BGS wars (well away from his THKI footprint) and it became pretty obvious that while he said he was "independent" what that actually meant was that he saw himself as being superior to the Imps and that he could manipulate the Imps to do his bidding. To achieve this, he had to get an in with Imperial High Command as a worthy and important ally, a big man, a man of power. So he jumped into a couple of BGS wars against us declaring that we had to give up control of systems to the Imp backed PMFs that had been expanded into the system.
