Reinforcement (Fortification)

How to defend and strengthen your Power's controlled systems.

Overview

What is reinforcement? Earning merits in your Power's controlled systems to increase their control score. Systems progress through three levels: Exploited → Fortified → Stronghold.
  • Control points = merits ÷ 4 (exploration/exobiology: merits ÷ 6)
  • ~333,333 CP per level, 1M CP maximum
  • Only one level can change per weekly cycle
  • Control scores decay each cycle (nonlinear — higher score = more decay), but no system loses its level from decay alone
-35% merit penalty applies to all reinforcement activities (since April 2025 rebalance). A dynamic modifier (-20% to +30%) also adjusts based on how much the system is being undermined.

Activity Details

Reboot Missions (1,800 merits)

On-foot settlement reboot missions. Spawn when the target faction is in Civil Unrest or Infrastructure Failure. Target settlement can be in the same system or a neighboring system.

For reinforcement only: uploading tracker malware and stealing power goods do not work alongside reboot missions for reinforcement purposes.

Megaship Datalink Scan (900 merits)

Find megaships as Points of Interest in your navigation filter. Scan the ship first, then the datalink at less than 200m distance. One scan per cycle per system. Megaship location changes each cycle.

Settlement Power Data (360 merits each)

Three types of data can be downloaded: Association, Classified, and Political. Downloading is illegal — may require illegal identities to access all data. Only some data is sellable in the same system; the rest may be tagged as out of range (known bug).

Selling Commodities at 40%+ Profit

Merit count is based on profit margin. Selling full loads at once is fine. Mid-price minerals like Gallite and Indite are good choices — low value commodities are easier to hit 40% but earn less income, while high value commodities are difficult to reach 40% profit.

Liberal Packages

Collect from Power Contact at any Winters Stronghold every 30 minutes. Starting allocation is 15, increasing with PP rank up to 250. Hand in at a Power Contact in the reinforcement system (cannot be the same stronghold). No fast-forward available.

Ethos & Tips

Winters' reinforcement ethos is Finance, giving +50% merits on finance-type activities: trade, mining, commodity hauling, exploration.

Winters Power bonus: +60% on food and medicine profits, +100% on minor faction reputation gain, +100% on salvage profits in controlled systems.
